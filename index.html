Bentley

Main Character / Author / Inventor

Bentley is the central character of the BentleyPlayz universe and the creator of the stories. He invents machines, explores portals, discovers magical artifacts, and leads scientific adventures.

Bentley also becomes the superhero Epic-Man during certain events.

Epic-Man

Superhero Identity of Bentley

Epic-Man is Bentley’s superhero form. When he transforms, he gains powerful abilities such as telekinesis and super strength to stop villains and protect people.

Zack the Wizard

Wizard / Magical Scientist

Zack combines magic with science. He creates magical technology, portal equipment, and powerful devices to help Bentley during dangerous adventures.

Bob

Scientist

Bob works with Bentley in the laboratory and helped develop the portal machine used to explore alternate universes and recover missing objects.

Supporting Scientists

  • Phil – Portal energy scientist who stabilizes dimensional travel.
  • Jerry – Technology engineer who builds tracking devices.
  • Gregory – Researcher studying alternate universes.
  • Fred – Electronics specialist maintaining lab power systems.

Cookie Man (Dan)

Villain

Cookie Man steals the Magic Football because he wants to create unlimited cookies. His ultimate goal is to produce infinite cookies for himself.

Courtroom Characters

Judge Chrissy – Presides over the courtroom case where Bentley is accused of abusing magic.

Dyric – The accuser who claims Bentley used magic improperly and presents the Magic Football as evidence.

Jaylin – Appears alongside Dyric during the case.

Judge Chrissy reviews the evidence and ultimately dismisses the case.

School Characters

  • Gracie – One of Bentley’s classmates and friends.
  • Syd – Appears in school stories with Bentley and Gracie.
  • Mr. Bevins – Bentley’s teacher.
  • Ms. Angie – School staff member present during the Halloween incident.
