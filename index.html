Characters from BentleyPlayz Stories & 2011Stories

Bentley Zack Epic-Man Bob Cookie Man Judge Chrissy Scientists

Bentley

Main Character / Creator

Bentley is the central character in the BentleyPlayz universe. He invents machines, explores portals, and discovers magical items such as the Magic Football. Bentley frequently works with scientists and magical allies to solve problems and stop villains.

Zack the Wizard

Wizard / Magical Scientist

Zack is a powerful wizard who creates magical items and advanced technology. He helps Bentley by building powerful equipment such as magic devices, portals, and superhero suits.

Epic-Man

Superhero Identity

Epic-Man is Bentley’s superhero form. With special powers like telekinesis and super strength, he protects people and stops villains across the BentleyPlayz universe.

Bob

Scientist

Bob works in the science lab with Bentley. He helps build the portal machine used to explore alternate universes and recover missing objects.

Cookie Man (Dan)

Villain / Cookie Lover

Cookie Man steals the Magic Football to create unlimited cookies. His main goal is to produce infinite cookies for himself.

Judge Chrissy

Judge

Judge Chrissy appears in the courtroom storyline where accusations are made about illegal magic. She reviews evidence and ultimately dismisses the case.

Supporting Scientists

  • Phil – Works on portal and energy systems.
  • Jerry – Develops tracking systems and technology.
  • Gregory – Assists in dimensional research.
  • Fred – Electronics and power systems specialist.
